Schulers Body Shop / Restoration

What We Do

We are a body shop dedicated to make what you need look its best. We have been in business for over 25 years.

There is no project we can't handle. We grind, bondo, sand, smooth-out, restore, paint, sandblast, and the list goes on. Everything from cars, trucks, tractors, and highway equipment, we restore and make it look brand new. We are family owned, community driven, and we LOVE what we do! Check out our gallery of our shop, before and afters, and our greatest assets, our family and those who work with us that is just like family.

Who We Are

The best team in the world.

We are a hard working family, with a special love for volleyball, soccer, basketball, and weight training. We are competitive on the field and in the body shop. Our work is 2nd to none.
